Client-Side Encryption

With our service, your files are encrypted and decrypted directly on your device. This means that only you and the recipient can access the content of the files. Our server never has access to your unencrypted files, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Hereâ€™s how it works:

  • Before uploading, your files are encrypted using AES-GCM with a 256-bit key, a state-of-the-art encryption standard.
  • Only the encrypted version of your files is uploaded to our server.
  • When downloading, the encrypted files are decrypted on your device using the decryption key you provided.

This client-side encryption model guarantees that your data remains private and secure, as it is never exposed to our server in an unencrypted form. You can trust that your sensitive information is safe with SecureSling.
